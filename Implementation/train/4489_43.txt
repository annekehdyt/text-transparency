`` el mar ''
directed by catalonian director agusti villarona ,
and based on the novel by blai bonet 
offers a glimpse of the spanish history
as seen by a balearic author
that takes the viewer back to the day of the civil war in that country .
the movie concentrates on three friend ,
and follows them from those early day during the onset of the war in majorca ,
to a few year later as two of those friend meet again
when they are at a sanatorium ,
lost in the countryside.we first meet three boy
that are playing happily .
not everything is what it seems .
the tragic death of one of them point out about the cruelty of the one
that commits the evil deed .
the boy have excluded a young girl 
about their age ,
from taking part in their game.when
we meet the adult ramallo again ,
he is on his way to a sanatorium .
he seems to be suffering from tuberculosis .
to his surprise 
manuel tur 
one of his boyhood friend is also being treated ,
and the young girl
that was not welcomed to participate in their game
is now one of the nun
that supervise their health care .
it is obvious
that tur look at ramallo in a way
that only means he is in love with the tough bully .
their relationship will have devastating consequences.roger casamajor does a good job with portraying the older ramallo .
bruno bergonzino makes an impression as tur 
the vulnerable youth .
antonia torren plays sor francisca with conviction .
angela molina 
puts an appearance as carman 
the wife of the caretaker of the institution .
simon andreu is perfect as alcan notara .
`` el mar '' is a dark film
that clearly shows agusti villarona 's talent
in making the novel come
alive for the viewer .
